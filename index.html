Welcome to the Storypath System

This character creation tool will guide you through creating a unique character with a rich backstory.

You'll select from three paths:

  • Legacy Path - Where your character comes from
  • Bond Path - An impactful person in your character's life
  • Catalyst Path - The event that set them forward on their journey

For each path, you'll choose 3 cards from 20 possibilities, then select one final card that best represents your character.
